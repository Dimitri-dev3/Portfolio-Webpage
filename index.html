About Me

My name is Dimitri Liapis and I love programming. My favorite thing about programming is the building aspect of it. You put all of these little peices together to make something work. I especially like game devlopment and coding up the different functions and watching the characters and sprites move. It is like being digital Bob Ross! Even as a young gamer I would always question how the developer put together the game. Like how does the A button have the ability to trigger 4 different Pokemon moves. Does it read the move name does the move hold data that plays once pressed. So this is how I would play games when I was younger and now that I am able to make games and that it is much more accessible than it was in 2004 when I was 7, I love to develop games in my free time.
